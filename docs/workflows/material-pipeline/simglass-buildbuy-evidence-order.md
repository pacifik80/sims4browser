## Evidence order

Use `Build/Buy SimGlass` evidence in this order:

1. external creator-facing `SimGlass` packet
2. local external tool packet
3. object-side transparent split
4. aggregate `Build/Buy` survey presence
5. narrowed `Build/Buy` route packet
6. reopened fixture evidence

## Safe weighting by evidence class

### 1. External `SimGlass` packet

What it can do:

- prove the family is real
- define the narrow transparent layered semantic floor

What it cannot do:

- prove that a `Build/Buy` transparent object belongs under the same branch automatically

### 2. Local external tool packet

What it can do:

- prove that external tooling preserves `SimGlass` as a real branch
- prove that the branch matters operationally in preview/export logic

What it cannot do:

- close `Build/Buy` family identity by itself

### 3. Object-side transparent split

What it can do:

- define the stronger competing explanations that `Build/Buy` must beat before `SimGlass` can win

What it cannot do:

- prove that `SimGlass` never appears in `Build/Buy`

### 4. Aggregate `Build/Buy` survey presence

What it can do:

- keep `Build/Buy SimGlass` alive as a bounded possibility

What it cannot do:

- promote a fixture
- close a family reading

### 5. Narrowed route packet

What it can do:

- rank search anchors
- define the best next reopen order

What it cannot do:

- substitute for reopenable fixture evidence

### 6. Reopened fixture evidence

What it can do:

- decide whether the branch loses, stays provisional, or wins

What it must still obey:

- evidence ceiling
- promotion gate
- disqualifiers
- positive signals
- outcome ladder
- provisional or winning checklists

## Compact safe rule

For `Build/Buy SimGlass`:

- survey keeps the branch alive
- route ranks the next fixture
- reopen decides the branch
